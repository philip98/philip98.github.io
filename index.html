Philip Schlösser

Welcome to my homepage. I am a maths PhD candidate at Radboud University Nijmegen under Erik Koelink and Maarten van Pruijssen working on matrix spherical functions and their relationship to orthogonal polynomials.

Besides that I am an enthusiastic choir singer, twice-participant in the Dutch Student Chamber Choir, board member of the student choir NSKAD, and editor of their choir newspaper.
