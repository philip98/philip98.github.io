Philip Schlösser

Welcome to my homepage. I am a maths PhD candidate at Radboud University Nijmegen under Erik Koelink and Maarten van Pruijssen working on matrix spherical functions and their relationship to orthogonal polynomials. On 24 August 2026, at 16:30 CEST I will defend my thesis entitled ‘Intermediate Macdonald Polynomials and Where to Find Them’.

Besides that I am an enthusiastic choir singer, thrice-participant in the Dutch Student Chamber Choir, former board member of the student choir NSKAD, and editor of their choir newspaper.
